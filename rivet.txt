
								----Rivet----

1. Robust Independent Validation of Experiment and Theory (Rivet) is a toolkit used as a method of validating theory by the use Monte Carlo (MC) event generators.

2. It has a host of various analyses, and has the ability to write your own analyses. 

3. Rivet is used for comparison and development of new theoretical models.

4. It has a framework written in OO C++, with a python interface.
